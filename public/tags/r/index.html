In the current paradigm, laboratories, especially clinical laboratories are designed to be data producers. When I say “clinical laboratory” I am referring to laboratories that have very limited reasearch functions. These type of laboratories typically provide services for hospitals, clinics or is a referrence lab for specialized testing. These laboratories churn out thousands of columns and millions of rows of structured data daily, yet most of it remains unused within the laboratory.
